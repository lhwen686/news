Unlike traditional gyms, Life Time facilities integrate expansive training areas with pools, tennis courts, group classes, spa offerings, and broader lifestyle services, positioning the brand as an all-in-one wellness destination rather than a simple workout venue.

Instead of pursuing aggressive unit expansion, the company prioritizes maximizing the productivity of its roughly 170 existing locations.
