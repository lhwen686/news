Original Text
Internal medicine training (IMT) is the principal entry route to physician specialities in the UK.1 Yet, shortlisting metrics are drifting beyond what can reasonably be expected at this career stage. In round 1 of 2026 IMT recruitment, 8251 applications were considered for shortlisting in England, Northern Ireland, and Wales.2 The median score of these applications was 21 (maximum 35), an increase from 15 points in 2025.2 Scores are self-assessed across portfolio domains and, for 2026, include an additional five points for applicants who apply only to the internal medicine vacancy.
