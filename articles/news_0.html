儿童罕见肝肿瘤起源的新见解

New insight into origins of rare liver tumors in children

该发现对儿童肝癌的临床诊断和治疗产生了深远影响。首先，HBC作为独立肿瘤类别的确认及其较差的生存率，强调了精准分类和早期识别的重要性。其次，WNT信号通路被确定为关键驱动因素，这为开发靶向HBC的新型疗法提供了明确的分子靶点。通过抑制WNT信号，有望促进肿瘤细胞分化，提高其对现有化疗的敏感性，从而改善HBC患儿的预后，推动个体化精准医疗的发展。
Original Text
Liver cancer in children is rare, but when it occurs the two main types are hepatoblastoma (HB) and hepatocellular carcinoma (HCC). In some cases, however, the tumors show features of both types. These tumors have been classified into a third tumor category called hepatoblastoma with carcinoma features (HBC).

There are many unanswered questions about tumors in this category, but in a current study published in the Journal of Hepatology, researchers at Baylor College of Medicine, Texas Children's Hospital and collaborating institutions reveal valuable insights into HBC's cellular composition and the relationships between different cell types in the tumors. The findings have implications for the tumors' response to therapies and understanding how HBC tumors emerge.

In 2022, our team identified HBCs as a third group of liver tumor types in children. In the current study, we applied multiple genetic profiling techniques to characterize the composition of 42 HBCs, mapping out the origins of the different cell types and their responses to treatment." Dr. Pavel Sumazin, co-corresponding author, professor of pediatrics, hematology-oncology at Baylor

Sumazin also is a member of the Dan L Duncan Comprehensive Cancer Center and Quantitative and Computational Biosciences at Baylor

Using DNA and RNA single-cell sequencing – a technique to study thousands of tumor cells one at a time – the researchers discovered that HBC tumors contain three kinds of cancer cells: HB-like cells, HCC-like cells and HBC-specific cells, which show molecular features of both HBs and HCCs. "HBCs originate from hepatic stem cells and these types of undifferentiated tumor cells are less responsive to chemotherapy and immunotherapy," Sumazin said.

Analyzing treatment outcome data from 41 patients revealed that children with HBC tumors had worse survival rates than children with typical HB. "The 5-year overall survival is about 80% for HB patients and around 40% for HBC patients overall, but transplanted HBC patients do better," Sumazin said. "This significant difference underscores the importance of understanding what makes HBC tumors so resistant to treatment."

When the team investigated the relationships between the different molecular cell types in HBC tumors, they discovered that HBC cells originate from HB progenitors and can transition toward HCC, highlighting a molecular continuum between HB and HCC.

"These transitions (HB → HBC → HCC) occur during early embryonic stages of liver development," Sumazin said. "HBC tumors tend to be composed of cells that have undergone differentiation arrest and retain molecular and other characteristic features of early hepatic stem cells."

These HB → HBC → HCC transitions occur when the WNT signaling pathway malfunctions. "This pathway plays a crucial role early in normal liver development: it is very active in stem cells then it typically quiets down to allow for cells to differentiate," Sumazin said. "But in all HBC tumors, WNT stays active and prevents cells from developing normally. Inhibiting WNT signaling promoted cell differentiation and enhanced sensitivity to chemotherapy."

The study shows that the process that leads to HBC tumors is dynamic and involves multiple HB-to-HBC and HBC-to-HCC transitions. "We showed that HBCs do not arise from a single progenitor cell but instead descend from multiple HB cells that independently undergo tumor-type transitions into HBC."

Together, these findings provide a new developmental model of pediatric liver cancer, linking sustained WNT signaling during early liver development to tumor heterogeneity, tumor-type transitions and patient outcomes.

儿童肝癌非常罕见，但一旦发生，主要有两种类型：肝母细胞瘤（HB）和肝细胞癌（HCC）。然而，在某些病例中，肿瘤表现出两种类型的特征。这些肿瘤已被归类为第三种肿瘤类别，称为伴癌性特征的肝母细胞瘤（HBC）。

对于这一类肿瘤，仍有许多未解之谜。但在《肝病学杂志》发表的一项最新研究中，贝勒医学院、德克萨斯儿童医院及合作机构的研究人员揭示了HBC细胞构成的宝贵见解，以及肿瘤中不同细胞类型之间的关系。这些发现对肿瘤对治疗的反应以及理解HBC肿瘤如何产生具有重要意义。

“2022年，我们的团队将HBCs确定为儿童肝肿瘤类型的第三个类别。在当前这项研究中，我们应用多种基因图谱分析技术来表征42个HBC的构成，描绘出不同细胞类型的起源及其对治疗的反应。”贝勒医学院儿科、血液肿瘤学教授、共同通讯作者Pavel Sumazin博士说。

Sumazin还在贝勒医学院丹·L·邓肯综合癌症中心以及定量与计算生物科学部任职。

研究人员利用DNA和RNA单细胞测序（一种一次研究数千个肿瘤细胞的技术）发现，HBC肿瘤含有三种癌细胞：肝母细胞瘤样细胞、肝细胞癌样细胞和HBC特异性细胞，这些细胞表现出HB和HCC的分子特征。“HBCs起源于肝干细胞，这些未分化肿瘤细胞对化疗和免疫疗法不敏感，”Sumazin说。

分析41名患者的治疗结果数据显示，HBC肿瘤患儿的总生存率低于典型HB患儿。“HB患者的5年总生存率约为80%，而HBC患者的整体生存率约为40%，但接受移植的HBC患者预后较好，”Sumazin说。“这一显著差异突显了理解HBC肿瘤为何如此耐药的重要性。”

当研究团队调查HBC肿瘤中不同分子细胞类型之间的关系时，他们发现HBC细胞起源于肝母细胞瘤祖细胞，并可以向肝细胞癌转化，这突出了HB和HCC之间存在一个分子连续体。

“这些转化（HB → HBC → HCC）发生在肝脏发育的早期胚胎阶段，”Sumazin说。“HBC肿瘤通常由经历分化停滞并保留早期肝干细胞分子和其他特征的细胞组成。”

这些HB → HBC → HCC转化发生在WNT信号通路功能异常时。“这条通路在正常肝脏发育早期发挥着关键作用：它在干细胞中非常活跃，然后通常会逐渐平静下来，以允许细胞分化，”Sumazin说。“但在所有HBC肿瘤中，WNT始终保持活跃，阻止细胞正常发育。抑制WNT信号可促进细胞分化并增强对化疗的敏感性。”

该研究表明，导致HBC肿瘤发生的过程是动态的，涉及多个HB到HBC以及HBC到HCC的转化。“我们发现HBC并非源于单一祖细胞，而是由多个独立的HB细胞转化成HBC。”

总而言之，这些发现提供了一个新的儿童肝癌发育模型，将肝脏早期发育过程中持续的WNT信号与肿瘤异质性、肿瘤类型转化和患者预后联系起来。
