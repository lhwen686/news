多巴胺包装功能障碍如何加速帕金森病症状进展

How dysfunctional packaging of dopamine advances symptoms of Parkinson's disease

该研究揭示了帕金森病（PD）发病机制中的一个关键新环节：中脑多巴胺能神经元内多巴胺（DA）包装异常。传统上，PD以多巴胺能神经元丢失和α-突触核蛋白聚集成路易小体为特征。本研究发现，囊泡单胺转运体2（VMAT2）介导的DA向突触小泡的异常包装是引发神经毒性过程的关键。具体而言，细胞能量载体腺苷三磷酸（ATP）供应不足以及VMAT2表达可能降低，均会损害VMAT2对DA的有效封装。未被封装的细胞质DA随即氧化，产生有毒物质和活性氧，直接损伤神经元。更重要的是，这些氧化产物可促进α-突触核蛋白错误折叠并病理性积累，形成恶性循环，加速神经元死亡。研究证实，简单地补充ATP即可修复DA包装并阻止神经元损伤，为PD提供了重要的治疗启示。这一发现将VMAT2功能和细胞生物能量学确立为PD干预的新靶点，有望突破现有以症状管理为主的治疗局限。此外，基于诱导多能干细胞（iPSC）的疾病建模，将极大地加速药物筛选和患者特异性疗法的临床转化进程。

Deficient uptake of DA into synaptic vesicles of human midbrain neurons results in DA oxidation and α-synuclein pathology. Credit: Science Advances (2026). DOI: 10.1126/sciadv.adz5645

A new study shows how dysfunctional packaging of the neurotransmitter dopamine triggers toxic processes in neurons—and how this can be repaired with simple delivery of energy (ATP). Parkinson's gradually destroys dopamine-producing neurons in a specific area of the midbrain, causing tremors, stiffness, and movement problems. Two key features are the accumulation of the protein α-synuclein into Lewy bodies and the loss of dopaminergic neurons.

"Dopamine oxidizes to produce toxic substances and causes lasting damage to the neurons if it is not properly packaged in small bubbles, known as vesicles—but the cause of this dysfunctional packaging of dopamine was hitherto unclear," says Lena Burbulla, Professor of Metabolic Biochemistry in the Faculty of Medicine at LMU and member of the SyNergy Cluster of Excellence. She is the senior author of a study published in the journal Science Advances.

Neurons from stem cells placed under the microscope

The researchers converted induced pluripotent stem cells (iPSCs) from a Parkinson's patient with a defective DJ-1 gene and genetically modified iPSCs lacking the DJ-1 gene into neurons.

"The lack of DJ-1 causes energy problems that occur in many variants of Parkinson's," explains Burbulla. Using high-precision protein analysis (proteomics), state-of-the-art imaging and sensitive dopamine sensors, the team of researchers revealed how dopamine is "packaged" incorrectly in the cells.

Dopamine becomes a cell toxin

The protein VMAT2, which is responsible for the secure packaging of dopamine into the vesicle, does not function properly in Parkinson's neurons: It does not take up enough dopamine—firstly because there is a lack of energy in the form of ATP (adenosine triphosphate), the universal energy carrier in cells, and secondly because the neuron does not produce enough VMAT2.

As a result, dopamine ultimately oxidizes to form toxins. Another crucial factor here is that misfolded α-synuclein protein accumulates—probably a consequence of the oxidized dopamine, which can bind proteins and promote their accumulation.

As the researchers were able to show, simple delivery of ATP repaired the packaging of dopamine and stopped the damage.

Therapeutic potential of dopamine packaging

"This discovery links an energy deficiency to the packaging of dopamine and neuron vulnerability—a new mechanism for Parkinson's," says Burbulla. It shows that intact VMAT2 and secure packaging of dopamine are key factors for protecting midbrain neurons and preserving them could slow down the pathology.

"iPSC-based disease modeling will enable future therapy tests to be conducted directly in patient cells and will accelerate translation from laboratory to clinic."

人类中脑神经元突触小泡对多巴胺（DA）摄取不足，导致多巴胺氧化和α-突触核蛋白病理。图片来源：《科学进展》（2026）。DOI: 10.1126/sciadv.adz5645

一项新研究揭示了神经递质多巴胺的异常包装如何在神经元中引发毒性过程——以及如何通过简单地提供能量（ATP）来修复这一问题。帕金森病会逐渐破坏中脑特定区域产生多巴胺的神经元，导致震颤、僵硬和运动障碍。其两个关键特征是α-突触核蛋白在路易小体中的积累以及多巴胺能神经元的丢失。

“如果多巴胺未能正确包装在被称为囊泡的小泡中，它就会氧化产生有毒物质，并对神经元造成持久损害——但这种多巴胺异常包装的原因迄今尚不清楚，”慕尼黑大学医学院代谢生物化学教授、SyNergy卓越集群成员Lena Burbulla说。她是发表在《科学进展》杂志上的一项研究的资深作者。

显微镜下的干细胞神经元

研究人员将一名患有缺陷DJ-1基因的帕金森病患者的诱导多能干细胞（iPSCs）以及基因改造后缺乏DJ-1基因的iPSCs转化为神经元。

“DJ-1的缺失会导致在许多帕金森病变体中出现的能量问题，”Burbulla解释说。通过高精度蛋白质分析（蛋白质组学）、最先进的成像技术和灵敏的多巴胺传感器，研究团队揭示了多巴胺在细胞中如何“错误包装”。

多巴胺成为细胞毒素

负责将多巴胺安全包装到囊泡中的蛋白质VMAT2，在帕金森病神经元中功能不正常：它无法摄取足够的多巴胺——首先是因为细胞中缺乏通用能量载体腺苷三磷酸（ATP）形式的能量，其次是因为神经元没有产生足够的VMAT2。

结果，多巴胺最终氧化形成毒素。另一个关键因素是，错误折叠的α-突触核蛋白积累——这很可能是氧化多巴胺的后果，氧化多巴胺可以结合蛋白质并促进其积累。

正如研究人员所能证明的，简单地提供ATP修复了多巴胺的包装并阻止了损害。

多巴胺包装的治疗潜力

“这一发现将能量缺乏与多巴胺的包装和神经元脆弱性联系起来——这是帕金森病的一个新机制，”Burbulla说。它表明完整的VMAT2和多巴胺的安全包装是保护中脑神经元的关键因素，而对其的保存可能减缓病理进展。

基于iPSC的疾病建模将使未来的治疗测试能够直接在患者细胞中进行，并加速从实验室到临床的转化。
